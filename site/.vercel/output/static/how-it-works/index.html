How it works

From "yes please" to your doorstep in four simple steps

We've kept ordering simple and quick — and you'll see and approve your design before we ever go to print.

  1. 1 🛒

    Choose your cheque

    Pick Single Use or Reusable, and select your size — Mini, Maxi or Mega — then check out securely online with Stripe.

  2. 2 ✏️

    Tell us the details

    We’ll email you almost instantly to collect the key details for your cheque: payee name, amount, message, logo and website address.

  3. 3 👍

    Approve your proof

    Our in-house design team creates an artwork proof of your cheque and sends it to you for feedback and sign-off — nothing goes to print without your okay.

  4. 4 🚚

    We print & dispatch

    Once approved, your cheque is manufactured within 3-5 days and sent by courier on a signed-for, next-working-day service.
