Cookies

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer or mobile device that uniquely identify your browser. Your consent to our use of cookies is required for key parts of this website to function properly.

What are cookies used for?

Cookies let a site recognise repeat visits, understand how the site is used, help you navigate between pages, remember your preferences, and generally improve your browsing experience.

What types of cookies does Mega Cheques use?

  • Strictly necessary cookies — essential for the site and checkout to function, including secure payment via Stripe.
  • Performance cookies — help us understand how the site is used so we can improve it (e.g. anonymous analytics).
  • Functionality cookies — remember your preferences between visits.
  • Targeting cookies — used sparingly, if at all, and only with your consent, to make any advertising more relevant.

How long do cookies stay on my device?

Session cookies last only until you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain until they expire or you delete them.

First and third-party cookies

First-party cookies belong to Mega Cheques. Third-party cookies may be set by services we rely on, such as Stripe for secure payment processing.

Managing cookies

Most browsers let you view, manage, delete and block cookies. If you disable cookies, parts of this site — including checkout — may not work properly. See allaboutcookies.org for general guidance on managing cookies in your browser.
